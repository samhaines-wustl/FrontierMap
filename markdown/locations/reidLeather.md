# Ariel Reid's Leather Co.

*A small tanning company just south of the Barnburn Hills*

### People
 - **Ariel Reid**: An ambitious halfling ready to strike out on her own with her leather works. However, with every deal she makes, a darkness looms behind her eyes.
 
 - **Johnny 'Railroad' Sampson**: A seasoned hide hunter and Reid's go to, Sampson is a mountain of an orc who knows the Nokomont Midlands like the back of his hand.

*There's an devilish miasmia in this tanning parlor, and its not just the uncovered brining tanks*
